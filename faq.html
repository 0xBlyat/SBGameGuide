FAQ

Use the search bar
The search bar at the top of the Swagbucks website is very convenient. If any offer still exists, then you can find it using the search bar. If you still can't find it, then the offer has been removed. It might be added back later, so keep an eye out. Also please inform me so that I can remove the offer.

$1 = 100SB
Every SB is essentially a penny. I've written the payout amounts in USD just to make it easier to read.

Offer amounts are always changing
I try to keep up, but sometimes offers keep changing rapidly. Sorry in advance.

Contact Swagbucks support
I am not a Swagbucks employee, so I can't help with anything. Your best option is to find the offer in the 'visited' section of your ledger, and to open a ticket from there.
